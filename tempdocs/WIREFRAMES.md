## Control Bar States

```
IDLE state:
  [⟲ Reset]  [🔀 Randomize]  [⏮ ----]  [▶ Play]
  (step disabled — nothing to step through)

PLAYING state:
  [⟲ Reset]  [🔀 --------]  [⏮ ----]  [⏸ Pause]
  (randomize & step disabled)

PAUSED state:
  [⟲ Reset]  [🔀 --------]  [⏮ Step]  [▶ Play]
  (randomize disabled, step enabled)

COMPLETE state:
  [⟲ Reset]  [🔀 Randomize]  [⏮ ----]  [▶ ----]
  (only reset & randomize enabled)
```
